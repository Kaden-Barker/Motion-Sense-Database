Trigger Execution

Author: Kaden Barker

This page provides an example interface to show that the workoutLog_BEFORE_INSERT trigger is working correctly.

Description:

The before insert trigger on the workoutLog table enfores that an athlete will not complete a log for a workout that isn't available on the date entered.



Justification:

Workouts are provided over a specific time inorder to produce results. It is important that athletes are only entering logs for workouts that are availble for that date range.



Execution Examples:

In the real world the athlete would know what workout there on and would also know what workout plan that they just completed. In this representation try creating a workoutLog. Set the workoutLog number to 11, the workoutPlan number to 1, and the date to 2023-02-07. After clicking submit you will see that an error occured. This is because the trigger caught that the date entered is out of the range of the workout plan. Now change the completion date to 2023-01-07. As you can see the entry was successfull.
